Straight answers, before you even ask.

The questions overseas buyers ask me most about shipping from China to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and India.

How long does ocean freight from China to Vietnam take?

For FCL to Hai Phong and Ho Chi Minh (Cat Lai), sailings usually arrive in under one week.

How long does shipping from China to Iraq (Umm Qasr) take?

Up to about six weeks. In current conditions almost all cargo to Umm Qasr is transshipped through a Gulf hub such as Khor Fakkan, Sohar or Fujairah before the final leg.

What is an FOB nominated forwarder, and why does it matter?

When you buy on FOB terms you have the right to choose your own forwarder in China. Named as your nominee, I work for you — not the factory — coordinating the supplier, the booking, export customs and the documents, and keeping you updated in your time zone.

What kind of cargo do you handle?

Mostly small appliances and project cargo. Note that most carriers do not currently accept dangerous goods on the China–Iraq lane.
