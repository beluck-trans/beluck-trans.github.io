China to Iraq

Umm Qasr is the destination; in today's conditions almost everything routes through a Gulf transshipment hub — Khor Fakkan, Sohar or Fujairah — before the final leg. That extra step is exactly where shipments get stuck, so it's where I pay the most attention: I keep the transshipment moving and tell you the truth about timing.

  • Destination Umm Qasr
  • Transship via Khor Fakkan · Sohar · Fujairah
  • Transit Up to ~6 weeks
  • Modes FCL · FOB nominee · DDP / DAP
  • Note Most carriers don't accept dangerous goods on this lane right now.
